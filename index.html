Intersections
Speculative

Design is a relation tool. It's a tool that I hope to use to build adjacencies and expand the adjacent possible. Design can be used to dictate behaviours, but I am interested in design as a collaborative act, even in static contexts. This perspective is informed by open source philosophy, cybernetics and post-modern thought. My practice is one of listening. I seek to design as becoming, allowing genorocity to exist as a foundational element of praxis. In sum, these impulses are centered around a desire to explore the infrastructure of communication and to deliver executable experiences.

Actionable

I believe design is a global practice. It cannot remain isolated in the studio, but instead must find its place within a broader discourse, alongside journalism, academia and advertising. As such, It’s not enough for design to address current events. Design must insert itself into the action, in order to document, analyze and influence. Design, at its heart, is a tool of social interface. This must be recognized and integrated into every step of the design process. All this must be informed by a strict vision, bound by syntax and systems of triage.
