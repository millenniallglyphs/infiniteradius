Hi, I'm Calvin!

design / writing / speculation

I believe design is a global practice. It cannot remain isolated in the studio, but instead must find its place within a broader discourse, alongside journalism, academia and advertising. As such, It’s not enough for design to address current events. Rather, design must insert itself into the action, in order to document, analyze and perhaps even to influence. I’m interested in how data, content, graphics, and interface come together to tell stories. I take a multidisciplinary approach to my practice, valuing risk and experimentation–using every opportunity to force my practice into conversation with world events.
