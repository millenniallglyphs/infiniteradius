AUTONOMOUS FITNESS UNION

develop anti-capitalist organization though fitness, community organization, and branding interventions.

Starting Points

Prompted to develop a new sportsware brand based on a market insight, I found inspiration in a book titled "Antifascism, Sports and Sobriety." Detailing the important role sports unions played in developing socialist solidarity in Austria, during the Seccond International period, I decide to ask what would a similar organization would look like today? Given the prompt, my investigations began with branding interventions on clothing, creating ad-hoc uniforms for members of this group. This expanded to a possible guerilla advertising campaign, and ultimately resulted in the protyping of a sports app as union.

Rhizomaic Brand Propagation

Ignoring the conventions of clothing labels, Rhizome seeks to overgrow existing brands through user-member intervention. Having joined the sports union, a member will be sent patches and labels to afix to the activewear they already own. Doing so rhizome creates possibility for a number of outcomes, including the development of (unwilling) brand partnerships, building arawness of systems impact, distribution of a brand in environmentally consciousness manner, propogation in viral fashion, and the staging of aesthetic coups.

Uniforms as Praxis

Wearing a Rhizome garment brings awareness to the Union’s presence and offers visual confirmation of anticapitalist activity. Training, exercising and building discipline are the characteristics of a revolutionary force. As such, participating in fitness activities while wearing this garment can be viewed as an action and a form of praxis.

Branding in Practice

Despite the fact that this brand would be largely propogated by individuals without formal design training, I compiled brand guidelines, including both specs and procedural instructions. Participants are recomended to apply the patches on dark items of clothing to maximize the effect and to build a unifying characteristic.

Guerilla Marketing

As an intervention and a marketing strategy, Rhizome aims to overrunn advertising spaces–absence being its message. This is manifest in the reclaimation of comercial spaces through the elimination of visible branding.

App as Fitness Union

Participate in community hosted training, or make those gains on your own. Rhizome makes it easy to do both providing an events based platform, as well as the tools to track goals and structure work outs. Build solidarity while you build stamina. Increase your V02 max while developing action networks within your own community.

Community Connection

Find people in your community to train with. Build anticapitalist systems of solidarity while getting action ready. When the time comes, you’ll be ready for what ever comes your way. Participate in community self defence, build a runner league, all from you phone.

Radical Fitnees Pracitice

Monitor your goals, build regements and track your metrics through Rhizome. See how each work out contributes to over all goals. Track runs, monitor personal bests and stay on schedule. Rhizome helps you stay organized, so you know exactly what you’re doing everytime you workout.
